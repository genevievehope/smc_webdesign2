Our Mission

St. Margaret's Center's mission is to provide relief, dignity and support to low-income people in crisis and to assist individuals through case management and skills development to become more self-sufficient and to achieve economic security.

We provide food programs including groceries, sack lunches, and senior food distribution. We also assist with utilities, immigration processing, counseling services, adult education classes ( ESL, Citizenship, GED), adult and child tutoring, CalFresh and MediCal application assistance, homeless programs, mobile showers, health screening, workshops, and holiday charities.
